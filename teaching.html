I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) affiliated with the Vision, Learning, and Control (VLC) group in the School of Electronics and Computer Science (ECS) at the University of Southampton.

I specialize in computer vision and machine learning in the context of artificial general intelligence. My research explores how machine learning can be improved by understanding how humans learn, and how it can, in turn, support human beings. This includes work on developing artificial machine intelligence with generative AI capabilities on generation, detection, emotion, and action in computers, devices, and robots. These technologies have broad potential applications in healthcare, the arts, and education.
